Services

LinkedHub connects task creators with available, skilled individuals in real time, offering smart matching, live availability, and AI-powered productivity. It streamlines task posting, assignment, tracking, communication, and payment all in one platform.

Task Posting & Matching

Users can easily post tasks by providing details like description, deadline, and required skills. LinkedHub instantly matches these tasks with available individuals who have the right skills to get the job done efficiently.

Live Availability System

live availability system that shows which users are currently online and ready to take on tasks. This allows task creators to assign work instantly, reducing delays and improving workflow efficiency

Skill-Based Filtering

allows users to filter available taskers based on specific skills like writing, design, or research. This ensures tasks are matched with the most qualified individuals, leading to better results and faster completion.

Ratings and Reviews

task is completed, both task creators and doers can leave ratings and reviews. This helps build trust within the platform and allows users to choose reliable, high-quality collaborators for future tasks.

Payment Integration

offers optional payment integration, allowing users to track task-based payments securely within the platform. It supports smooth transactions through trusted gateways, ensuring timely and transparent payments for completed work.
